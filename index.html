About Us

A quaint café located on the coast of Maine, serving coffee, espresso drinks, smoothies, sandwiches and delicious baked treats from Barn Door Baking Co.

Come enjoy an outstanding cup of coffee, espresso, latte, hot cider, cocoa, chai, tea -- your choice -- to accompany a delicious treat baked by Barn Door Baking Co. or a savory panini prepared by barista/owner Jim Matel and his amazing staff.

We are family run and serve food suitable for dairy and gluten sensitivities. Make sure to let us know whatever you need, and we'll be happy to find a way to make it work.
